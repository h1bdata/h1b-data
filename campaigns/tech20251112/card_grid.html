A Shared Frontier
Many of the advances in modern computing grew from the work of immigrant Indian engineers and researchers who came to the United States and found an environment that encouraged experimentation, collaboration, and ambitious ideas.
Working alongside their American colleagues in labs, universities, and companies, they helped shape progress in distributed systems, machine learning, generative AI, and robotics.
This timeline highlights those contributions - from first-generation Indian immigrants to second-generation Indian American innovators - and the shared efforts that moved these technologies forward.
